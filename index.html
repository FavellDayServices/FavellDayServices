What we do

A variety of activities are available to join in during the day.

This year our activities have included cooking, flower arranging, crafts, music making, pub lunches and some interesting talks by visitors.

Each day there is a group crossword and quizzes.

There is the opportunity to swim in a warm therapy pool (limited spaces) and also use of gym equipment, including passive exercise bikes.

Hot drinks are provided throughout the day and also a hot or cold midday meal.

The staff and volunteers are very friendly and always on hand for a chat and a helping hand with activities and general care.

Who We Are

Favell Day Services was registered as a Charitable Incorporated Organisation under registration number 1162011 on the 4th June 2015 having operated from January 2014 with oversight from Northamptonshire Health Charitable Fund whilst the formal charity registration process was progressed.

The charity objective is to provide or assist in the provision of facilities for recreation or other leisure time occupation for persons over the age of 18 who have need thereof by reason of a physical disability with the object of improving their life experience and living conditions.

It's current area of operation is Northampton and has ought to replace the day service previously offered by Northamptonshire Health Foundation Trust until service funding was withdrawn at Favell House.

Members of the charity are asked to pay for the service they receive while the chaarity seeks to augment this income with donations, legacies and community fundraising activities.

The care service specifically seeks to provive an enjoyable care environment for its members while also providing a break for carers and family members
